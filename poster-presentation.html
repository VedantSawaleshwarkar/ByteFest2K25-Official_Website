General Rules – Poster Exhibition

  • Participants must carry a valid college ID card.
  • Participation can be individual or in teams of up to 2 members.
  • Posters must be based on a technical/scientific topic relevant to the theme of the exhibition.
  • Posters should be in A2 or A1 size and must be printed or neatly hand-drawn.
  • Each team must explain their poster to the judges during evaluation.
  • Use of offensive, plagiarized, or unrelated content will lead to disqualification.
  • All content on the poster must be original and properly cited where applicable.
  • Participants must arrive 30 minutes before the scheduled start for setup.
  • Judges' decisions will be final and binding in all respects.
  • Best posters will be selected based on creativity, clarity, content, and presentation skills.

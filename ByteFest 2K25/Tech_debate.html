Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team must consist of 4 members.
  • College ID-card is mandatory for all participants.
  • Topics will be disclosed 5 minutes before the debate round.
  • Each debate will be between two teams: one speaking FOR and the other AGAINST the topic.
  • All team members must participate actively during the debate.
  • Use of mobile phones or internet resources is strictly prohibited during the debate.
  • Teams will be judged on clarity of thought, logical reasoning, rebuttal skills, communication, and teamwork.
  • Disrespectful or offensive behavior towards other participants or judges will lead to disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
